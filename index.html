About Us

More Than a Club

Infinity Basketball Club is a competitive program for kids and teens in La Crete, Alberta — dedicated to developing basketball skills, teamwork, and game strategy while competing in tournaments across Northern Alberta.

🏀

Competitive Play

We compete in tournaments throughout Northern Alberta, giving players real game experience against top competition.

💪

Skill Development

Twice-weekly practices led by experienced coaches focused on fundamentals, strategy, and athletic development.

🤝

Teamwork First

We build character on and off the court through a culture of respect, hustle, and support for every player.

🎓

Youth Focused

Programs designed for every stage from U11 through High School, with both boys and girls divisions.
