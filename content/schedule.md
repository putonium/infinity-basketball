Tryouts begin the week of **March 17**. Signup deadline is **March 18**. Players must attend a minimum of **3 of 4** tryout sessions to be considered.

To sign up for tryouts, email [infinitylacrete@gmail.com](mailto:infinitylacrete@gmail.com).
